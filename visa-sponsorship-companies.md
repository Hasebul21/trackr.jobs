# Visa-Sponsorship Companies for International SWEs

> Companies known to sponsor work visas / relocation for **international software
> engineers** in **Thailand, Malaysia, Japan, and Singapore**. Compiled from web
> research (June 2026). Sponsorship policies change per-role and per-quarter —
> always confirm "visa sponsorship / relocation provided" on the actual job post
> before applying.

**Visa types per country:**
- 🇯🇵 Japan — *Engineer / Specialist in Humanities* work visa (employer-sponsored); fast-track via Highly Skilled Professional (HSP) points.
- 🇸🇬 Singapore — *Employment Pass (EP)* (min. SGD 5,000/mo, SGD 5,500 finance, 2025); *Tech.Pass* for senior talent.
- 🇹🇭 Thailand — *Non-Immigrant B visa* + work permit (employer-sponsored); SMART Visa for tech.
- 🇲🇾 Malaysia — *Employment Pass (EP)*; MDEC *Tech Professional Pass* for the digital sector.

> ⚠️ ByteDance / TikTok in Singapore have recently **prioritized candidates who
> already hold the right to work** and prefer not to sponsor — verify per posting.
